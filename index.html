About Us

Our Story

Jaydeep Industries has grown to become one of Gujarat's premier manufacturers and suppliers of high-quality maize products. Located on Rajkot Highway in Shekhpar, we've built our reputation on consistent quality, innovative processing techniques, and exceptional customer service.

Our Expertise

With over 15 years of experience in maize processing, we specialize in producing a wide range of premium maize derivatives using state-of-the-art machinery and hygienic processes. Our products include corn grits in various sizes, corn flour, flaking grits, and animal feed supplements that meet the highest standards for food, feed, and industrial applications.
